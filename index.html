Key Focus Areas:

  • Expanded presidential authority and centralized power
  • Deregulation across industries
  • Conservative social policies
  • Reductions in federal spending and workforce
  • Immigration enforcement
  • Energy independence and fossil fuel expansion

üîÄ Project 2025 vs. Agenda 47

‚ñº

Comparison Overview: Project 2025 is a detailed 900+ page conservative blueprint from the Heritage Foundation, while Agenda 47 is Trump's high-level 2024 campaign platform. Despite campaign distancing, they align significantly (~60-70% overlap), with many Project 2025 authors in Trump's administration.

Key Similarities

  • Executive Power: Reinstate Schedule F; centralize authority; dismantle "deep state" agencies
  • Social Issues: End DEI programs; recognize only two sexes; ban transgender military service/care for minors
  • Education: Abolish Dept. of Education; promote school choice/vouchers; ban CRT/gender ideology
  • Economy/Energy: Deregulate; tax cuts; boost fossil fuels; roll back green policies
  • Immigration: Mass deportations; end sanctuary cities; deploy military for border security
  • Foreign Policy: Cut foreign aid; America First isolationism; end "woke" globalism

Key Differences

Project 2025 (More Detailed/Radical)

  • Comprehensive civil service overhaul (50K+ loyalists)
  • Full abortion funding ban nationwide
  • Eliminate Head Start/Title I
  • Replace income tax with consumption tax
  • Weaponize schools/churches for immigration enforcement

Agenda 47 (Broader/More Extreme)

  • Impoundment to bypass Congress; prosecute "enemies within"
  • Defund schools with vaccine mandates; "baby bonuses"
  • Create free online "American Academy"
  • Universal tariffs (10-20% on imports, 60% on China)
  • End birthright citizenship via EO; troops to Mexico

Implementation Overlap (as of Oct 4, 2025)

Many Agenda 47 promises advance via Project 2025-aligned actions (~48% of P2025 completed). Both agendas share progress on:

  • Executive Power: Schedule F reinstated, hiring freeze, religious accommodations (Completed Jan-Sep 2025)
  • Social Issues: DEI termination, two-sex policy, transgender bans (Completed Jan-Jul 2025)
  • Economy: IRS cuts, green fund halts, deregulation (Completed Jul-Sep 2025)
  • Immigration: Detention mandates, refugee suspension, border deployment (Completed Jan-Feb 2025)
  • Foreign Policy: Aid freeze, NPR/PBS defunding, Mexico City Policy (Completed Jul-Sep 2025)

Project 2025 provides the "how-to" playbook, while Agenda 47 sets Trump's rhetorical tone. Full alignment could reach 70% by 2026 if shutdown ends.
