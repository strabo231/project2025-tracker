📋 What is Project 2025?

▼
Important Context: This analysis tracks verifiable executive actions, laws, and proposals based on independent trackers and government records.

Project 2025, formally titled Mandate for Leadership: The Conservative Promise, is a 900+ page policy blueprint published in April 2023 by the Heritage Foundation and over 100 conservative organizations.

Four Main Pillars:

  • Personnel Database: A database of political appointees for rapid staffing
  • Policy Agenda: Comprehensive proposals across 34 federal agencies
  • Training Academy: Educational program for conservative appointees
  • 180-Day Playbook: Rapid implementation strategy

Key Focus Areas:

  • Expanded presidential authority and centralized power
  • Deregulation across industries
  • Conservative social policies
  • Reductions in federal spending and workforce
  • Immigration enforcement
  • Energy independence and fossil fuel expansion
